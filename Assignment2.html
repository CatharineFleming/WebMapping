Highest Peaks in Oregon:

The three highest peaks in Oregon are Mount Hood, Mount Jefferson, and South Sister.
These mountains are all a part of the Cascade Range.


Mount Hood:

Mount Hood is 11,249 ft tall. This is the tallest peak in Oregon.

Mount Jefferson: & South Sister:

Mount Jefferson is 10,502 ft tall. This is the second highest peak in Oregon. South Sister is 10,363 ft tall. This is the third highest peak in Oregon.
